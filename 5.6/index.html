Our Story

Luke and Katie Kenney are the proud owners of Blackbird Cafe. Luke and Katie have resided in Groton, MA for nearly ten years where they are raising their three children.


Their dream to open a cafe that offers the highest quality and freshest coffee, tea, baked goods and sandwiches around became a reality in December 2010 with the opening of Blackbird Cafe in Groton.


Active in the local community, they are proud to be part of the Groton small business community and serve local products, including George Howell"s Terroir Coffee, roasted in Acton. There is always something new and interesting to check out at Blackbird Cafe.
