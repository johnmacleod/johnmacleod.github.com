Gliders of World War II: "The Bastards No One Wanted"
Major Michael Manion's thesis about combat gliders of World War II. The thesis begins with an appropriate quote from Walter Cronkite. My father was assigned to the combat glider program soon after joining the army. He and some of his fellow pilots were fortunate to move out of the combat glider program.

Rougham Tower Association
The Rougham Tower Association is located near Bury St. Edmunds in the UK. This group has preserved the tower used by the 8th Airforce 94th Bomb Group in World War II. I visited it in 1999 with my dad and his fellow airmen. The local members from the Rougham Tower Association treated the returning airmen as heroes 50 years later.

Wilber Richardson, Ball Turret Gunner
I was prividged to meet Mr. Richardson on the 1999 trip to the UK. He was a ball turret gunner in a B17. He was extensively interviewed by the History Channel.
