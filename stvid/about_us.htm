About Us

TECHNOLOGY

We can supply your desired footage in Beta-SP, or Mini DV tape, or we can digitize and upload footage for a client’s use.


CITYSCAPES

Our collection encompasses every conceivable category, from business and medical, to scenics, cityscapes and lifestyles. Master elements are available overnight on all video and digital formats.

ARCHIVAL

We have an impressively large archival collection, currently numbering over 8,000 prints, from the 20's, 30's, 40's and 50's as well as historical events of the 60's through 2000.

NEW ENGLAND

We have the definitive collection of New England images, both aerial and land, from the turn of the century to today!


NATURE

Our library also contains footage of nature, wildlife, everyday current events, sports, and time lapse material shot by some of the world's most renowned cinematographers.
