How Time Flies!

Thank you for visiting. It's hard to believe that we're approaching the end of 2003. The year has just gone by too fast! So, we have to stop and take a moment to thank all of our loyal customers who continue to call on us for their stock footage needs. Our deepest thanks to all of you. And if you're new to the world of stock footage, we hope you'll give us the opportunity to help with your next project. As we approach our 20th year in business, we remain one of the largest, privately owned stock footage companies in the nation. Our contemporary collection exceeds 10,000 hours and our vintage film collection has grown to over 8,000 prints from the early 1900's to the 70's.

We acquire new material on a regular basis, so please call or fax if you don't see what you're looking for in our Partial Stock Footage Subject Index.

WE NOW ACCEPT CREDIT CARDS are now acceptable forms of payment.

QUICKTIME: Clips are now available in a QuickTime format. For those of you who are pressed for time, we can e-mail motion files or stills for preview purposes. This works well in situations where just one or two clips are needed. Feel free to call us with any questions.

FOR SALE: A large collection of 8mm and Super 8mm films. If you're a collector of old 8mm films, you'll find some interesting and even rare items. Visit our 8MM AND SUPER 8 MM FILM TITLES INDEX. There are over 1100 titles to choose from ! Make an offer on one or all but please be patient when making inquiries. Researching the collection takes several days. Many thanks to all who have inquired so far. Please note that effective immediately, we have created a mirror site which features a frequently updated version of the 8mm database: www.mikehuard.com. Mike now personally handles all 8mm inquiries and sales.

WANTED: Vintage sports material. We'd prefer 16mm reels but will consider just about anything as long as the quality is acceptable. We're also still seeking any archival footage shot in and around Boston. 16mm, Standard8mm or Super8mm is OK. FInally, we're on the hunt for updated footage which features any and all aspects of current technology. Manufacturing, automation, computer technology or anything cutting edge would be great.

GOT FOOTAGE?

STOCK VIDEO continues to hunt for suppliers of fresh footage. If you own some interesting material and are interested in selling, we'd love to hear from you. We are especially interested in 16mm or 35mm sources but tape source is welcome, too. Give us a call at (617)323-4400 oremail us with any information about your footage.

We would really like to hear from you! We will assemble a Preview Reel of up to 10 minutes at no charge for anyone who mentions this site and the date of your visit. This offer is good for telephone inquiries only, please.
