NOTHING SACRED -Color, Super8, Sound. One of Ben Heckt's jaundiced views of society. Tough and gritty satire-style. Produced by David Selznick and directed by William Wellman in 1937. Stars Carol Lombard and Frederic March.

THE MAN ON THE EIFEL TOWER -b&w, Super8, Sound. A wily police inspector craftily breaks down the resistance of a murderer when evidence is lacking. An intelligent, suspenseful melodrama directed by Burgess Meredith. Stars Charles Laughton and Franchot Tone. Produced in 1949. 1400 feet on two reels.

HIS GIRL FRIDAY -b&w, Super8, Sound. Directed by Howard Hawks, 1940. Stars Cary Grant. A farce of love and competition. One of the funniest comedies of its time. 2000 feet on two reels.

TOP HAT -b&w, Super8, Sound. 1935. Stars Fred Astaire and Ginger Rogers with music and lyrics by Irving Berlin. Complete set, 2400 feet on six reels in original Deluxe Edition box.

KOOK'S TOUR - b&w, Super8, Sound. The Three Stooges last film. Moe, Larry and Joe DeRita star in this 1970 pilot for The Stooges' last series. Larry suffered a stroke after filming and never recovered so the series never materialized. A Niles print, 400 feet in original plastic case. Highly collectible.

TWO TARS - b&w. Super8, Sound score. Hilarious Laurel & Hardy 1928 comedy. 400 feet, Blackhawk print in original box.

MURDER OF THE BASKERVILLES - b&w, Super8, Sound. A classic Sherlock Holmes mystery. 1600 ft. on four reels, Original Box, Blackhawk print.

VALLEY OF TERROR - b&w, Super8, Sound. A 1937 cattle rustlin' western starring Kermit Maynard. 1200 feet on three reels. Blackhawk print in original box.

INTOLERANCE - b&w, Standard8, Silent. The 1916 D.W. Griffith epic. 2000 feet on five reels. Nice print with original title and dialogue slates.
