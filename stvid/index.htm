Over ten thousand hours of footage, competitive rates and expert, personalized service makes Stock Video one of the video industry's oldest and most respected stock footage houses.

Stock Video licenses the use of stock footage to producers of commercial, industrial, educational, cable and documentary television programs and feature films. Master footage, collected from a variety of sources, is cataloged by computer and listed by a variety of categories including detailed subject matter, location, source, quality, length and format. Our collection encompasses the years 1900 thru 2000.

About Us

Our collection encompasses every conceivable category, from business and medical, to scenics, cityscapes and lifestyles. Master elements are available overnight on all video and digital formats. We have an impressively large archival collection, currently numbering over 8,000 prints, from the 20's, 30's, 40's and 50's as well as historical events of the 60's through 2000. We have the definitive collection of New England images, both aerial and land, from the turn of the century to today! Our library also contains footage of nature, wildlife, everyday current events, sports, and time lapse material shot by some of the world's most renowned cinematographers.
