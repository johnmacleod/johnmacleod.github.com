Welcome to Camp Maisy!

Too busy to exercise your dog? Let us do the work for you!

We’ll take your dog on a 3-mile minimum walk, off leash (unless other-wise advised). We have a very happy, fun-loving golden retriever and we love dogs of all shapes and sizes!

Does your dog need a day excursion, or an overnight camp experience?

With us, your dog can enjoy hiking, biking, playing with friends… even rollerblading! It’s all about fun for dogs!!!!!!

Camp Maisy gets your dog out-side, socializing with other dogs, and getting the exercise he/she needs and deserves.

You will feel better after your long day, knowing that your dog’s physical and emotional needs have been met.
